Background

Bonoful has been established with the aim to offer the people living in Sweden to taste Bangladeshi cuisines. It is a very popular brand in Bangladesh. Now the top management decided to expand the business all over the world, especially in Europe, USA, Australia and Middle East. It is not only because a lot of Bangladeshi citizens live in these areas either for work or study purposes but also the oriental spicy food is becoming more and more popular in these areas.

The main purpose of 'Bonophul restaurant' is not only doing business but also to create 'Bonoful' as a brand for Bangladeshi foods in these areas. Since, Bangladeshi foods are very rich in taste and we have some world class chefs and service personnel, we believe that we will be able to serve and entertain our customers with the best quality foods and services.
